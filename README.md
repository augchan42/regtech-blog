## About This Blog

This blog focuses on **regulatory literacy** — teaching the mechanics of how regulations work, not just what specific rules say.

Whether you're a compliance professional, a crypto founder, a lawyer, or just someone trying to understand why your exchange suddenly changed its policies — this series will help you understand the machinery behind the rules.
